This is my 1.1.9 project! Building a Chipotle Bowl!

Our project was based on Chipotle. We allowed the user to be able to create their own bowl using the choices of white or no rice, chicken or steak, salsa or no salsa, and lettuce or no lettuce. At the end the user is also asked here or to go in which, either choice they pick the text "Your bowl is done!" pops up on the screen.
